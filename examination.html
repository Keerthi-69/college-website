Examination Information

  • Exam Schedules: Check the upcoming exam dates for all semesters and departments.
    Download Exam Schedule (PDF)
  • Hall Tickets: Students can download their hall tickets from the student portal. Ensure you have your registration number handy.
  • Exam Guidelines: Read the rules and regulations to be followed during examinations.
    • Arrive at least 30 minutes before the exam starts.
    • Carry your hall ticket and valid ID card.
    • Mobile phones and other electronic devices are strictly prohibited.
  • Result Announcements: Results will be published on the official college website within 15 days after the completion of exams.
  • Revaluation Process: Students can apply for revaluation of answer sheets within 7 days of result announcement.
